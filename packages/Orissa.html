Orissa Tribal Tour

Destination

Bhubaneshwar - Konark - Puri-Gopalpur- Belgarh - Rayagada - Jeypore - Chitrakote - Jagdalpur -Narayanpur - Kanker - Raipur

Best Time to Visit : October - April

6 Days / 05 Nights Overview

Tribals of Orissa are still untouched by the civilization , the tribals of Orissa and Bastar are still forest dwellers and live in harmony with their natural surroundings. Their life is dependent on forest produce. Each tribe has its own customs , tradition and costumes which separate them from other tribal.

Highlights

Bhubaneshwar:Odissi dance

Puri: Sun Temple

Rayagada:Famous Swa beach in Bay of bangal

Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Bhubaneshwar

Morning after breakfast checkout from hotel and transfer to airport to board the flight to Bhubaneshwar. Up on arrival meet and assistance by our representative and transfer to hotel.
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02 :Bhubaneshwar - Konark - Puri

Morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Konark. On arrival visit Sun temple of Konark which is also known as the Black Pagoda. Continue driving to Puri. On arraival check in at hotel later in the evening visit Jagannath Temple from outside.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 :Puri - Gopalpur

Drive to Gopalpur-on-Sea. Its a famous sea beach in Bay of Bengal. Spend rest of the day at leisure. Overnight in Gopalpur
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Gopalpur on Sea
Morning after breakfast excursion of Baliguda during your excursion visit the different Villages of Desai Kondh Tribe. Evening return to hotel. Overnight in Gopalpur
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Rayagada
In the morning visit Chittakona Wednesday Market which is frequented by the DongriaKondhs, Bhumias tribes etc. While returning back visit DesiaKondhs village.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Jeypore
Day excursion to Onkadeli Market to visit Thursday weekly market. This market is visited by the most interesting tribes of Orissa, the Bondas. You can meet Bondas while they are coming to Market. Also visit Lamtaput village of Gadaba Tribe. Overnight in Jeypore.
Meals: Breakfast
