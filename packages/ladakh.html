Leh Ladakh Tour

Destination

Delhi -Srinagar - Kargil- Lamayuru- Alchi- Leh

Best Time to Visit : October - April

6 Days / 05 Nights Overview

Ladakh is a region in India totally isolated from the modern world. An authentic land, it is faithful to ancestral customs where life is characterized by intense spirituality. Even an Indian traveler will probably find no similarities in the land and people between the ones he leaves behind and those he encounters in Ladakh. .

Highlights

Srinagar:Sikara Ride on Dal Lake, Visit to the beautiful Gardens of Srinagar

Kargil:Passing through Zozila Pass

Alchi:Passing through Zozila Passr

LehWorld's highest motor pass, Hemis Monastery, Thiksey Monastery, Shey Monastery

Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Arrive Delhi

Our repersentative will receive you at the International airport and welcome you and escort you to your hotel.
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02: Srinagar

Morning after breakfast, drive up to Shankeracharya Temple dedicated to Shiva 1000 ft above Srinagar City an magnificent view from the top overlooking various lakes of Kashmir.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 :Kargil

Morning after breakfast start driving for Lamayuro and Alchi. On the way visit The Monastery of Lamayuru (125 kms from Leh, Lamayuru is a pretty little village surrounded by the rocky Hindukush peaks.The Lamayuru Gompa is about halfway between the verdent valley of Kashmir and the upland desert plateau of Tibet.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Alchi-Leh
After breakfast check out the hotel and first visit The Alchi Monastery Around 76 Kilometers west of Leh, south of the Indus River, is the little village of Alchi. It's one of the most beautiful villages in Ladakh and an ideal place to get a feel for Ladakhi village life.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Leh
Morning after breakfast, we will take you to a guided tour of the Monasteries of the Indus Valley. Your excursion would start with **Witness the Morning prayer ceremony at Shey Monastery is (15 Kms South of Leh), Hemis Monastery is (45 Kms South of Leh), Thiksey Monastery is (25 Kms South of Leh).
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Leh-Delhi
After early breakfast our representative will assist for transfer to airport to board the flight for Delhi. After arriving at Delhi airport board the flight to onward destination.
Meals: Breakfast
