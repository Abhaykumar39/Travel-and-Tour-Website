splendors of Kerala

Destination

Cochin - Munnar - Periyar - Allepey - Kovalam

Best Time to Visit : October - April

6 Days / 05 Nights Overview

Sprawling mansions set in lovely locales-exotic islands, luxuriant game reserves… on hillsides, by beaches, backwaters…. That is what Kerala is all about it

Highlights

Cochin:Jewish Synagogue, Kathakal

Munnar: Tea Garden Estate

Periyar:Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Allepey:Houseboat

Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Cochin

Welcome to India - a country where hospitality is a way of life; to a culture steeped in tradition, a land where history lives in music and dance. You will be received at the airport in a traditional manner and transfer to the hotel
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02 :Cochin-Munnar

Morning after breakfast you will taken for full Day sightseeing of Cochin. Visiting the Dutch palace, the Jewish synagogue (closed on every Friday, Saturday and Public holidays), St. Francis church. Also view the Chinese fishing nets. Relax in the afternoon.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 : Munnar

Tour to Kundala Lake, Tea Musuem, spice plantations, tea gardens, Mathupetty and other interesting tourist destinations on your Munnar travels. Travel to the Anaimudi Peak that is the highest point of the Western Ghats in Kerala, South India and enjoy wild verdant Kerala as you course wood trails in Munnar that lies about 1,600 meters above the sea level.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Periyar
Morning after early breakfast proceed for nature walk and later proceed to Elephant Junction to enjoy the day with elephant. One hour ride. Afternoon proceed to boat cruise on Periyar Lake, surrounded by forests meadows and hills. One can sight wildlife like elephants, woodland birds, darters and cormorants.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Allepay
Morning after breakfast proceed to Kovalam. Upon arrival, check-in at the hotel. It boasts of three mesmerizing crescent shaped beaches namely the Lighthouse beach, Hawa beach and Samudra beach. Overnight in Kovalam
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Kovalam
Continue your stay at the hotel & spent time at leisure for own activities at hotel. Overnight in Kovalam.
Meals: Breakfast
