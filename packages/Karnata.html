Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Bangalore

Morning after breakfast go for half day city tour with visit to the Vidhan Sabha that houses the State's Legislature and Secretariat, the Nandi Temple that is one of oldest temples in India and is popularly known as the Bull temple
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02 :Bangalore-Coorg

Morning after breakfast, we transfer you to Coorg. Kodagu also known by the name Coorg is an affluent hill station endowed with alluring natural beauty and charm. It is an ideal gateway that provides excellent opportunity to be with the nature and feel its various exciting facades.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 : Coorg

After a healthy breakfast, Today Enroute we visit to Maidikeri Fort and then proceed forward to visit Raja's Seat, Nagarhole National Park, Iruppu Falls, Abbey Falls and the holy Omkareshwara Temple Overnight in Gopalpur
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Kabini
This morning, embark on a boat safari, getting to know the many species of this region from a new vantage point. Afternoon time at leisure. Overnight in Kabini.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Mysore
Today after breakfast, transfer you to Mysore. Later by evening visit Brindavan Gardens - where you will explore the beauty of flowers and evening musical light show.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Mysore-Hassan
After breakfast, we proceed for Hassan. The oldest town of Hoysala Empire of 11th to 13th century AD in India. Visit the temples of Halebid which are the silent witness to the rich cultural heritage of Karnataka.
Meals: Breakfast
