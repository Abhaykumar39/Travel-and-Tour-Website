Dream in Shimla Tour

Destination

Delhi - Agra - Jaipur - Shimla -Delhi

Best Time to Visit : October - April

6 Days / 05 Nights Overview

Golden Triangle with Shimla tour. Shimla, the summer capital of the British India, is situated at a height of 2,196 meters. While the British have left the echoes linger on. today, its well developed facilities, easy accessibility and many attractions make it one of India's most popular resorts

Highlights

Delhi:Old City, Chandni Chowk, Jama Masjid, Rajghat, India Gate, Parliament Building.

Agra:Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri

Jaipur:Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Amber For

Shimla:Kufri, Jakhu Temple, Hanuman Temple,

Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Arrive Delhi

Our repersentative will receive you at the International airport and welcome you and escort you to your hotel.
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02 Delhi-Jaipur Surface

Morning , drive down to Jaipur. Jaipur is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Rajasthan. It was founded on 18 November 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II. Upon arrival check in at hotel
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 :Jaipur

Morning after breakfast we will drive to Amber Fort The classical romantic Rajasthan Fort palace. It's construction was started by Man Singh I in 1592 and completed by his descendant Jai Singh-I.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Jaipur/ Agra Via Fatehpur Sikri Surface
Morning after breakfast, proceed to Agra. Agra is one of the prominent destinations of the World and a notable landmark of India. En route visit Fatehpur Sikri 40 kms west of Agra, is built a city predominantly in Red Sandstone and is called Fatehpur Sikri
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Agra
Morning we will take you to the mesmerising Taj Mahal - known as the monument to love. The romantic story of this exceptional building is almost as beautiful as the Taj itself
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Shimla
Morning after relaxed breakfast, proceed for full day sightseeing tour to Shimla visiting Viceroy Lodge, Jhakhoo Hills. Later by evening you will take stroll down through Mall road
Meals: Breakfast
