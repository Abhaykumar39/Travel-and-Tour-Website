Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Delhi

Our representative will receive you at the International airport and escort you to your hotel. Overnight in Delhi.
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02 :Delhi - Amritsar

After breakfast check-out from hotel transfer to New Delhi railway station to board train to Amritsar Shatabdi. Arrive Amritsar and transfer to the hotel. In the afternoon proceed to Wagha Border - the international border between India and Pakistan.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 : Amritsar

Morning after relaxed breakfast, visit the Golden Temple - the holiest shrine of the Sikh religion, also known as Hari Mandir, Jallianwala Bagh - The park commemorates the 2000 Indians who were killed or wounded at this site, shot indiscriminately by the British in 1919
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Dalhausie - Khajjiar - Chamba
Morning after brekafast transfer to Chamba. En route stop at Khajjiar. It's a very beautiful meadow which is often referred as 'Mini Switzerland of India'. Later drive to Chamba. On arrival check-in at hotel
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Chamba - Nurpur - Dharamshala
Morning after breakfast transfer to Dharamsala via Nurpur, Visit Krishna Temple at Nurpur, Continue drive to Dharamshala. Upon arrival at check-into hotel for overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Dharamsala
Morning after breakfast proceed to The private Residence of the Dalai Lama, Tibetan Buddhist monastery and the museum which throws light on the rich heritage of the Tibetans.
Meals: Breakfast
