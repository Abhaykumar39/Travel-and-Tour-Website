Rajasthan Festival Tour

Destination

Delhi - Jaipur - Jodhpur - Pokhran - Jaisalmer

Best Time to Visit : October - April

6 Days / 05 Nights Overview

Rajasthan is a land of immense culture and heritage. Rich culture and traditions, along with beautiful landscapes. The Rajasthan Cultural Tours and Rajasthan Wildlife Tour is the perfect way to explore this incredible state. The package typically includes a visit to the major cities of Rajasthan such as Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer..

Highlights

Bikaner:Visit the famous Junagarh Fort

Jaisalmer:Camel ride and sightseeing

Pushkar:Famous Fair (Camel Fair)

Jodhpur: Spend some time in Mehrangarh Fort

Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Jaipur

After having breakfast, a you will be escorted on a tour of Amer Fort. In the evening visit Chokhi Dhani and experience the manifesting Rural Cultural and tasteful spread of Rajasthani delicacies served in the indigenous style.
Meals: Breakfast


Day 02: Jaipur-Jodgpur

Jodhpur is the second-largest city in the Indian state of Rajasthan and officially the second metropolitan city of the state. Morning after breakfast, checkout from the hotel and drive to Jodhpur.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 :Jodhpur-Pokhran-Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, nicknamed “The Golden city”, is a city in the Indian state of Rajasthan. Morning after breakfast, you will be driven to Jaisalmer.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Jaisalmer
In the morning, after breakfast, visit the Jaisalmer Fort. Also known as the 'Sonar Quila'. After that, you will visit the famous Havelis of Jaisalmer including the Nathmalji-Ki-Haveli.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Jaisalmer-Bikaner
Morning after breakfast, you will be driven to Bikaner and check in at your hotel. In the afternoon you will be taken for tour of Bikaner city, visiting magnificent forts, the Junagarh Fort Junagarh Fort And the Lal Garh Palace.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Pushkar
Pushkar is very famous because the Camel fair happens every year in the month of November. Morning, after breakfast.
Meals: Breakfast
