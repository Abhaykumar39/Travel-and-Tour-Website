Assam-Cruise Tour

Destination

Kolkata - Jorhat - Majuli - Jorhat - Kaziranga - Tezpur - Barpeta - Manas- Guawahati -Kolkata

Best Time to Visit : October - April

8 Days / 07 Nights Overview

Brahmaputra cruises feature visits and attractions such as wildlife viewing village walks, visits to tea gardens,exploring country towns in cycle rickshaws, barbecues on deserted river islands, dance performances, and visits to craft workshops.

Highlights

kolkata:Flower Market, Howrah Bridge, The Victoria Memorial, The Saint Paul'S Cathedral Botanical Garden

Majuli:Majuli Island, Paalnaam, Salmara Village

Kaziranga: Elephant Ride, Kaziranga National Park

Guawahati: Kamakhya Temple, Mathanguri Poin

Day Wise Plan

Day 01 : Arrive Kolkata

On your arrival at Delhi airport, you will be met by outside the customs enclosure and escorted till your check in at the hotel. Overnight in Kolkata
Meals: None


Day 02 : Kolkatai

Morning after breakfast visit the picturesque flower market of Calcutta. After a short walk across the famous Howrah Bridge you will take a Ferry to the north of the city to an interesting artist's quarter.
Meals: Breakfast



Day 03 : Kolkata - Jorhat -Majuli

Morning after breakfast. well in time checkout from the hotel and transfer to airport toboard the flight to Jorhat. Our representative will receive you at the airport and transfer to Nimati Ghat (Closest bank to Brahmaputra on Jorhat side).
Meals: Breakfast



Day 04 : Majuli
Today, we will explore the rest of the important sites of Majuli covering Auniati Satra Founded by Niranjan Pathakdeva, the satra is famous for the “Paalnaam” (Monastery Prayer) and Apsara Dances (Dance of the Angels) and also its extensive assortment of ancient Assamese artefacts, utensils, jewellery and handicrafts.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 05 : Majuli-jorhat-kaziranga
Morning after breakfast, take your Ferry back to Nimati Ghat. Upon arrival, proceed to Kaziranga (Kaziranga is one of the largest Protected Areas in India and one of the most significant conserved forest home to One-Horned Rhinoceros (Rhinoceros unicornis). On arrival Check in at hotel. Post lunch proceed for a safari to Kaziranga National Park. Overnight in Kaziranga
Meals: Breakfast

Day 06 : Kaziranga
Early morning at 0630 Hrs. proceed for an hour of Elephant Ride inside Kaziranga National Park. Post ride return to lodge for breakfast. Afterwards proceed for the Jeep Safari to the Western Range of the Park.
Meals: Breakfast

Day 7: Kaziranga-Manas
Post breakfast drive down to Manas National Park. The only Tiger Reserve in Assam. Upon arrival check-in at your Jungle Lodge. Overnight in Manas
Meals: Breakfast

Day 8: Manhas-Guwahati
Morning after breakfast, we would take you to a guided walk to Mathanguri Point on the Bhutan Side in search of golden Langurs. Post lunch drive down to Guwahati. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel.
Meals: Breakfast
